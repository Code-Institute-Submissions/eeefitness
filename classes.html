EEE Classes

Where Fun and Fitness Meet!

Pilates

The art of controlled movements. Pilates improves flexibility, builds strength and develops control and endurance in the entire body. It puts emphasis on alignment, breathing, developing a strong core, and improving coordination and balance.

Kettlebells

Typical kettlebell exercises build strength and endurance, particularly in the lower back, legs, and shoulders, and increase grip strength. The basic movements, such as the swing, snatch, and the clean and jerk, engage the entire body at once. A full-body workout.

Spinning

These classes focus on endurance, strength, intervals, high intensity and recovery. Our well-trained instructors uses music, motivation, visualization and enthusiastic coaching to lead you through a routine that best suits your fitness level and goals.

Zumba

If you like to dance but hate to work out, Zumba is perfect for you. It is an aerobic dance and fitness workout utilises Latin beats to get your body moving. This is a total-body cardio and aerobic workout.

Yoga

Our yoga classes incorporate systematic exercises and self-development techniques for body, mind and spirit. After gentle warm-up exercises our qualified yogis will guide you through a series of postures, flowing sequences, breathing exercises which will work your entire body, burn fat, and tone muscle.

Boxercise

Boxercise is a high intensity interval training class based on boxing training. Classes involve shadow-boxing, skipping, hitting pads, kicking punchbags, press-ups, shuttle-runs and sit-ups. It is a fun, challenging and safe workout which is great for stress busting and accessible to all ages and fitness levels.
